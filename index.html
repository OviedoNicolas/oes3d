About Us

At OES3D, we are passionate about pushing the boundaries of innovation through cutting-edge 3D scanning and reverse engineering solutions. Our team is dedicated to providing high-quality, comprehensive services to our clients.

Our Mission

Our mission at OES3D is to provide precise, accurate, and reliable 3D scanning and reverse engineering services. We are dedicated to transforming your ideas, designs, and products into practical, real-world solutions. With a strong emphasis on technological advancement and an unwavering commitment to excellence, OES3D is your trusted partner in the field of 3D scanning and reverse engineering.

3D Scanning

Utilizing state of the art industrial scanning equiment, we captures the shape, size, and appearance of real-world objects or environments to create a three-dimensional digital representation.

Reverse Engineering

Reverse engineering is the process of extracting the design intent from a 3D scanned mesh data and turning it into an accurate dimensional CAD drawing ready for manufacturing.
